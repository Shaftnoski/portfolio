My Favorite Superheroes: A Semantic Webpage

Marvel Heroes

Marvel Superhero comics are the best.
The storylines are more complex, and often address real life social issues. The MCU is far superior to the DC films. My favorite heroes are The X-Men, though the movies -previously produced by Fox- have traditionally been only decent.

DC Heroes (and Others)

DC Characters are good too, but the movies are rushed and badly written with lousy character development. Although, the animated movies and TV series they make are phenominal.

In summary, DC does great on TV but their live action movies are weak.

Marvel on the other hand produces gold regularly in all formats and have a vastly more interesting universe.
